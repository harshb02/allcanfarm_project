Our initiative is to bridge the gap between farmers and the professionals living in cities who want to contribute to farming but are not able to. With India’s focus turning back towards the farming sector that we have seen during the 2020 budget of Nirmala Sitaraman – we want to digitalize the farming sector in our capacity. By connecting the farmers and professionals – we reduce the risk of farmers. CORE VALUES– 1.Caring for people. 2.Integrity including intellectual honesty, openness, fairness & trust. 3.Commitment to excellence. These professionals maintain effective communication links and work in close coordination with each other.

Farmer

  • Reduce initial capital risk.
  • Get a helping hand during the growing period of the farm -this is the period for farmers where there is no revenue for regular care till produce is ripe.
  • Can earn additional revenue by hosting their professional partner in when they come and visit the farm.
  • Debt-free farming for farmers.

PROFESSIONALS

  • They reduce their carbon footprint.
  • Add farm income in their overall portfolio of stocks, mutual funds, property, etc.
  • In a Farmer’s Shoes learn new things.

There is no easy way for professionals to include farming income right now without leaving their Day job. Our initiative is a humble step forward in this direction
